vikings

Vikings is a historical drama television series created and
written by Michael Hirst for the History channel, a Canadian
network. Filmed in Ireland, it premiered on March 3, 2013, in Canada.
The series concluded on December 30, 2020, when
the second half of the sixth season was released in its entirety on
Amazon Prime Video in Ireland, ahead of its
broadcast on History in Canada from January 1 to March 3, 2021. A sequel
series, titled Vikings: Valhalla, premiered on
Netflix on February 25, 2022.[1]
